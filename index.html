About Us

Welcome to Acitizens Ebony Events, your go-to platform for organizing and participating in exciting events at Acity.

We aim to bring students, faculty, and administration together through interactive and impactful events that foster community, creativity, and collaboration.

Whether you are a student looking to attend an event or an admin wanting to create one, our platform ensures a seamless experience.

  • For Users: Explore events, view descriptions, check available seats, and schedule your participation.
  • For Admins: Manage event creation, track registrations, and update event statuses with ease.

Thank you for being part of this community. Together, we can make every event memorable!
